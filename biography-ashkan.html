Ashkan Shakarami - Biography

I am a Senior Scientist and Postdoctoral Fellow in Artificial Intelligence (AI) at the University of Bonn (Universität Bonn). My research interests are AGI, Computer Vision, Neural Networks, Computer-Aided Diagnosis, Histopathology, and Ophthalmology. My office is located at the Medical Department (Universitätsklinikum Bonn: UKB)
